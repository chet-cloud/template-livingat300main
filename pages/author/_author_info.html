Susan Ainley, in a reversal of the usual trend, relocated to downtown Winnipeg nine years ago from Alberta. Walking and biking everywhere, she loves the convenience of living in the heart of the city, where she can start the day walking her dog through the Forks, go for brunch with pals, shop unique boutiques in the Exchange in the afternoon and finish the day enjoying any of the many festivals and cultural events that happen year-round. Her instagram follows her day-to-day celebration of everything great in downtown Winnipeg.
